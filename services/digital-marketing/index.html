Common questions

Good to know.

How long until SEO shows results?
Local SEO typically shows movement within two to four months, with compounding gains after that. Ads produce leads much faster — which is why we often run both: ads for now, SEO for later.
What's more important — SEO or ads?
They answer different questions. Ads buy immediate visibility; SEO builds an asset that keeps producing without per-click cost. The right mix depends on your margins, competition, and timeline — that's part of what we scope in a free consult.
How do you report results?
Monthly, in plain English: leads generated, cost per lead, rankings that moved, and what we're doing next. You'll never need a translator for your own marketing report.
