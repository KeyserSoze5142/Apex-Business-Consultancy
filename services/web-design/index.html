Common questions

Good to know.

How much does a website cost?
Every project is scoped individually and quoted flat and in writing before work begins — no hourly billing, no surprises. Cost depends on size, features, and content.
How long does it take to launch?
Most small business sites launch within a few weeks of kickoff. Timeline depends mainly on how quickly we can gather your content — photos, menus, service details.
Will I be able to update the site myself?
If you want to, yes — we can build on an easy-to-edit platform and train you. Prefer not to touch it? Our care plans handle updates, hosting, and security for you.
