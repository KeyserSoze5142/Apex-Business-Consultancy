Common questions

Good to know.

What kinds of tasks can actually be automated?
More than most owners expect: lead intake and routing, appointment scheduling, follow-up emails and texts, invoice generation, data entry between systems, report compilation, and customer onboarding are common wins. If a task is repetitive and rule-based, it's a candidate.
Will automation replace my staff?
No — it replaces the busywork your staff shouldn't be doing. The goal is capacity: your people spend their time on customers and growth instead of copy-paste admin.
How long does an automation project take?
Most first projects go from audit to live system in a few weeks. We start with the highest-ROI process, prove the result, then expand from there.
