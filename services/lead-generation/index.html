Common questions

Good to know.

How is this different from just running ads?
Ads create traffic; a lead generation system converts and keeps it. We build the landing pages, capture, qualification, and follow-up that turn attention into booked business — whether the traffic comes from ads, search, or referrals.
What happens to leads after they're captured?
They're scored, routed to the right person, and entered into an automatic follow-up sequence within seconds. You see everything in one pipeline, and nothing depends on someone remembering to reply.
Can this work with my existing website?
Usually, yes. We can bolt a capture-and-follow-up system onto most existing sites — and if the site itself is the bottleneck, our web design team can fix that too.
