Common questions

Good to know.

My data is a mess across five different tools. Is that a problem?
It's the normal starting point. The first phase of every engagement is exactly this: finding the data, cleaning it, and connecting it. You don't need to prepare anything.
What does a KPI dashboard actually show?
Whatever drives your business — typically revenue and margin trends, lead volume and sources, conversion rates, job or ticket profitability, and marketing cost per lead. We build it around your goals, not a template.
Is this only useful for bigger companies?
The opposite — small businesses feel bad decisions faster. Even a simple dashboard usually pays for itself the first time it reveals an underpriced service or an ad channel that isn't working.
